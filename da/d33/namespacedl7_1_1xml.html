◆ WhitespaceHandling

enum struct dl7::xml::WhitespaceHandling
strong

Specifies how whitespace should be handled when parsing the textual content of elements. Regardless of this setting, textual content that contains only whitespace will always be completely discarded.

Enumerator
Preserve 

All whitespace is preserved.

Replace 

All occurrences of tab, line feed, and carriage return are replaced with spaces.

Collapse 

Contiguous sequences of whitespace are collapsed to a single space; leading and trailing whitespace is stripped.

Default 

The default whitespace handling, tendentially identical to "preserve".
