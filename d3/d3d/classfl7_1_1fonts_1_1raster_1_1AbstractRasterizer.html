Detailed Description

Abstract class for rasterizing glyphs into images, for example as a "classic" bitmap, a signed distance field (SDF), or a multi-channel signed distance field (MSDF), and (optionally) calculating the positional offset of a rasterized image relative to the typographic origin of the glyph.
